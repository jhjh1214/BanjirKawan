## Strategic implications (what we changed)
1. **Systems-first sequencing.** The pitch now leads with the **system map + the
   last-metre leverage point** (Phase-1 language), and the working product lands as
   *evidence the leverage point is real* — not as the headline. Avoids the fatal
   "cool app, thin thinking" read in front of a systems dean.
2. **Name the intervention explicitly** as the highest-leverage, lowest-cost point
   ("the last metre"). That one sentence is what this panel scores highest.
3. **Product = our edge on Quality of Solution (25).** Most teams pitch concepts;
   we show a deployed, tested, live system with real numbers. Lean into it as proof.
4. **SEA transferability** (Johor → Jakarta → Medan valley) added — resonates with
   the immersion-trip theme and banks "generalizable structure" systems points.
5. **Deck is a real deliverable + demo fallback.** Every live-demo beat has an
   embedded screenshot/recording so a wifi/projector failure costs us nothing.
